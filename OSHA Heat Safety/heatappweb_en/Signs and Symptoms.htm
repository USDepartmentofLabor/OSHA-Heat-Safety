Illness: Signs and Symptoms
Illness Symptoms
Heat stroke
First Aid
  • Red, hot, dry skin or excessive sweating
  • Very high body temperature
  • Confusion
  • Seizures
  • Fainting
Heat exhaustion
First Aid
  • Cool, moist skin
  • Heavy sweating
  • Headache
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Dizziness
  • Light headedness
  • Weakness
  • Thirst
  • Irritability
  • Fast heart beat
Heat cramps
First Aid
  • Muscle spasms
  • Pain
  • Usually in abdomen, arms, or legs
Heat rash
First Aid
  • Clusters of red bumps on skin
  • Often appears on neck, upper chest, folds of skin
